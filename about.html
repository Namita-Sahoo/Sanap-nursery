Avinash Sanap

“Trust of Farmers — Generation to Generation.”

Mr. Avinash Sanap is the Managing Director of Sanap Hi-Tech Nursery (I) Pvt. Ltd. He oversees the daily operations of the nursery with dedication and focus. He strongly believes in a customer-centric approach, ensuring farmers receive reliable guidance. His hands-on experience at the grassroots level gives him a deep understanding of farmers’ struggles and aspirations. His approachable and down-to-earth style builds trust and encourages open communication.

Mr. Sanap is recognized for his humility and inclusive leadership, fostering collective growth. As both a family leader and company head, he has nurtured Sanap Hi-Tech Nursery into a strong, trusted institution. Trust and loyalty from the farming community are his highest forms of recognition, above awards or honors. His leadership reflects a deep commitment to the welfare of farmers. The company’s guiding motto, “Trust of Farmers – Generation to Generation”, embodies his philosophy and mission.

Mrs. Sheetal A. Sanap

“The labour of women farmers is not just family strength but a true asset to society.”

Mrs. Sheetal Avinash Sanap is the Director of Sanap Hi-Tech Nursery (I) Pvt. Ltd. Born into a farming family, she developed a passion for agriculture from an early age. Marriage brought her closer to farming life, deepening her understanding of farmers’ realities. She became especially aware of the unique challenges faced by women farmers.

Mrs. Sanap recognizes that women’s contributions to agriculture often go unacknowledged. She actively works to raise awareness about women farmers’ labor and their vital role. Alongside managing the nursery, she advocates for the rights and recognition of women in farming. Her humility and compassion strengthen bonds with the farming community, ensuring that women farmers’ labor is valued as a vital societal asset.

Ashutosh A. Sanap

“Transforming a long heritage into a modern, trusted nursery brand.”

Mr. Ashutosh Avinash Sanap is the Executive Director of Sanap Hi-Tech Nursery (I) Pvt. Ltd., combining humility with visionary leadership. A commerce graduate currently pursuing an MBA, he brings fresh ideas and a modern outlook to the nursery. He is determined to transform the nursery into one of India’s leading agricultural institutions within the next decade.

Despite the nursery’s long history, he identified gaps in growth and initiated strategic change. He closely studies agricultural challenges, climate change, market fluctuations, and evolving customer needs. His approach focuses on plant quality, strong supply chains, advanced technology, and skilled leadership. Under his guidance, Sanap Hi-Tech Nursery has regained a reputation for trust and quality while balancing tradition and innovation.

Om A. Sanap

“Uncompromising quality and innovation to build lasting farmer trust.”

Om Avinash Sanap, the third-generation leader of Sanap Hi-Tech Nursery (I) Pvt. Ltd., carries a passion for innovation, discipline, and positivity. A BBA graduate, he envisions scaling the nursery to a turnover of ₹100 crore and achieving international brand recognition. For him, the nursery is not merely a business, but a mission to build a strong culture of management.

He emphasizes open communication, organizational culture, and automation in production processes. Om maintains that uncompromising quality is the foundation of customer trust. His leadership is defined by fearlessness, humility, and a unique approach to challenges. By studying global agricultural practices, he brings innovations to the nursery, aiming to transform Sanap Nursery into a symbol of farmers’ trust and modern excellence.

OUR VISION , MISSION & CORE VALUES

Growing Dreams, Sustaining Generations

Vision

Sanap Hi-Tech Nursery Pvt. Ltd. strives to make agriculture sustainable by providing high-quality seedlings of fruits, vegetables, and flowers. With modern technology and research, we aim to strengthen India’s farming sector and help farmers compete globally with confidence.

Mission

By 2030, Sanap Hi-Tech Nursery aims to impact 1,00,000 farmers by supplying healthy seedlings and promoting self-reliance. Through technology, research, and marketing support, we ensure sustainable farming under the principle “Trusted by Farmers, Sustained for Generations.”

Core Values

At Sanap Hi-Tech Nursery, we stand for trust, consistent quality, and transparency in every service. We focus on building long-term, caring relations with farmers and believe their growth is our growth. We continue to deliver excellence that sustains generations of farmers.
